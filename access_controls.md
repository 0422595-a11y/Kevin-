What are subjects? Subjects are the people that are trying to access an object.
What are objects? Objects are the items that subjects try to get into. For example an computer in an object. 
What are rules? Rules are guidelines that subjects must follow and some rules are required to be able to access something like an object. 
Real life examples: Subject: Teacher that is trying to open an google doc. Object: An google doc. Rule: Students cannot run in the halls.
Privileged accounts need stronger protection because they have an bigger impact on the company. For example, if a privileged account gets hacked that hacker can do alot of damage due to the fact they have more privileges than others.
